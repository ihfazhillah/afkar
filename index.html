Vim Plugin pro-tips:

- make it small, big plugins will require a lot of maintenance, small plugins are much simpler to maintain,
- if something can be done via a command then it should be made as a command, do not force your mappings on users.

-- https://vimways.org/2019/writing-vim-plugin/
12.
